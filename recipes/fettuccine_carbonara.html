Fettuccine Carbonara

Description

This fettuccine carbonara is a delectable, mouth-watering pile of yummy goodness! I recommend a nice salad with it — that's all you will need for a complete meal.

    • ½ tablespoon and ⅙ teaspoon olive oil
      1 ⅓ shallots, diced
      ⅓ pound bacon, cut into strips
      ⅓ large onion, cut into thin strips
      ⅓ clove garlic, chopped
      ⅓ (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
      ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
      2 ½ tablespoons and ½ teaspoon heavy cream
      1 egg yolks
      salt and pepper to taste
